## ğŸ§© Problem Statement

### 70%+ of Indian MSMEs lack Market Intelligence.

Micro and small businesses in India (over 63 million) face critical challenges:

- **Lack of Timely Insights:** Missing critical market shifts, supply issues, and policy updates.
- **Instinct-Based Decisions:** Relying on word-of-mouth rather than data, leading to missed opportunities.
- **Complex Regulations:** Difficulty tracking HSN codes and compliance updates.
- **Language Barriers:** Limited access to national and global digital information.

**MarketPulse AI** addresses these issues by providing a targeted, multilingual, and intuitive solution to bridge the information gap.

## ğŸ§­ How It Works

1. **Data Ingestion**  
   Collects data from Public Market Data, PyTrends, and Mock APIs for News and Regulations.

2. **Preprocessing & Profile**  
   Creates MSME profiles via Udyam ID or reverse-tracks unregistered users.

3. **Core Analytics Engine**  
   Applies rule-based logic to detect emerging trends linked to HSN codes.

4. **Recommendation Engine**  
   Generates actionable tips, passed through a Mock Translation Engine.

5. **Alert Delivery**  
   Sends real-time alerts and account updates to the user interface.

6. **UI Visualization**  
   Displays insights, trend violations, and compliance status on a Streamlit Dashboard.
